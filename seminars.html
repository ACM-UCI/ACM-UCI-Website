Thursday, May 10th Low-tech Hacks: How to defeat a $831 lock for $1
Abstract:
Talking about security oftentimes means involving a bunch of fancy tools and technologies that you've probably never heard of before. This talk has none of that. We'll look at some of the easy yet not-so-obvious ways anyone can "hack the system" to cause chaos and destruction. You'll learn about some of the basic concepts in physical security and social engineering, and see how anyone can use those concepts for evil (and how to protect yourself).
